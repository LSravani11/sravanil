Explore Tourism in Andhra Pradesh

Discover the beautiful locations of Andhra Pradesh, from temples to beaches and stunning landscapes.

Popular Destinations

Some of the must-visit places in Andhra Pradesh include:

  • Tirupati - Famous for its Venkateswara Temple.
  • Araku Valley - Known for its scenic beauty and coffee plantations.
  • Visakhapatnam (Vizag) - A coastal city with beautiful beaches and hills.
  • Amaravati - The historic capital with ancient Buddhist sites.
